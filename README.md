# Introduction-to-Cybersecurity
## What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and data from hackers, malware, and unauthorized access.

## Why is Cybersecurity Important?

Cybersecurity helps keep personal information safe, protects businesses from attacks, and ensures online services remain secure and reliable.

## Three Examples of Cybersecurity in Everyday Life

1. Online banking protects financial transactions.

2. Hospitals secure patients' medical records.

3. Social media accounts use security to prevent unauthorized access.
